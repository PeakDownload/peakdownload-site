Overview

Built around the way real leagues actually run.

Too many leagues still rely on paper score sheets, manual spreadsheet updates, and disconnected communication between commissioners, captains, and players. That creates extra admin work, delayed standings, and very little visibility for the people actually participating each week.

LeagueCast replaces that patchwork with one connected system for score entry, standings, roster participation, schedules, and a more engaging competitive experience on league night.
