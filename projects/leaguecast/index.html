Overview

Built around the way real leagues actually run.

Too many leagues still rely on paper score sheets, manual spreadsheet updates, and disconnected communication between commissioners, captains, and players. That creates extra admin work, delayed standings, and very little visibility for the people participating each week.

LeagueCast is designed to replace that patchwork with one connected system for score entry, standings, schedules, roster participation, and a more engaging competitive experience on league night.

Why it exists

From paperwork and spreadsheet lag to a live competitive surface.

LeagueCast is driven by a simple premise: league night should feel like a live competitive experience, not an administrative chore.

01

Live score entry

Teams enter scores into one shared system instead of handing off paper and waiting for later updates.

02

Standings without delay

Results, standings, and match outcomes can update as the night unfolds instead of after manual spreadsheet work.

03

More visible participation

Commissioners, captains, and players all get a clearer, more engaging view of the league they are part of.
