FAQ

LeagueCast questions.

What is LeagueCast?

LeagueCast is a live recreational league platform for scores, schedules, matchups, standings, player results, and league-night coverage.

Is LeagueCast only for bowling?

Bowling is the first supported sport. LeagueCast was designed as a broader multi-sport scoring platform, with golf, darts, cornhole, pickleball, and other recreational league formats in view as it grows.

Can LeagueCast support our scoring format?

LeagueCast supports structured, league-specific scoring rather than forcing every league into one generic model. Contact PeakDownload to evaluate custom scoring options for your league.

Can teams compete from different locations?

Yes. Teams can bowl at different locations while scores, matchups, and standings remain synchronized.

When will LeagueCast be available?

LeagueCast is currently in private alpha testing, with public launch planned for Fall 2026.
