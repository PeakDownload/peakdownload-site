Bowling League App

Vertical SaaS Sports & Recreation In Development

Bowling leagues run on paper score sheets, disconnected spreadsheets, and manual reconciliation. League secretaries spend hours on data entry. Players have no visibility into standings or history. Center operators cannot see their own business clearly.

The Bowling League App replaces that patchwork. One system for league scheduling, live scoring, player statistics, and center analytics — built around the actual workflows of bowling centers, not adapted from generic sports software.

Designed for real-time display on overhead monitors. Accessible to players and spectators from any device during league night.
