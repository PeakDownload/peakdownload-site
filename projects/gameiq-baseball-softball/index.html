FAQ

Common questions.

Is Game IQ for baseball or softball?

Both. The app teaches shared fundamentals and is named to include both baseball and softball families.

What age is it best for?

It is designed primarily for beginner youth players, especially around ages 8–12, while remaining useful for parents and brand-new fans.

Is this a scoring app?

No. Game IQ is a learning app focused on understanding the game, not managing live games or teams.

Does it require an account?

The current product direction does not require accounts for the beginner learning experience.
