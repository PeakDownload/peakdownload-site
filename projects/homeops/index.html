Overview

Built for the information homeowners always mean to organize later.

Important home information usually ends up fragmented across emails, paper folders, photos, notes, PDFs, and memory. That makes it harder to stay ahead of maintenance, understand what was serviced, and make better replacement decisions over time.

HomeOps gives homeowners a single operating layer for the practical side of ownership — what you have, how it is doing, what is due next, and what it may cost later.

Operational clarity across the life of a home.

Product direction

More than a tracker.
