HomeOps

Vertical SaaS Home Management In Development

Homeownership has an invisible maintenance workload: HVAC filters, gutter cycles, roof inspections, appliance warranties, seasonal prep. Most of it goes untracked until something fails. Failures are expensive.

HomeOps brings structure to home maintenance. It tracks what your home needs, when it needs it, and who did the work. A complete service history, vendor directory, and scheduling system in one place — built for homeowners who want to stay ahead of problems, not respond to them.

Think of it as a maintenance operating system for your home.
