Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

PeakDownload LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate our products and communicate with you. We do not sell your data.

Information We Collect

When you sign up for early access or product updates on any project page, we collect:

  • Email address — provided voluntarily when you submit a signup form.

We do not collect names, phone numbers, payment information, or other personal data through our marketing site.

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address solely to:

  • Send product launch announcements for the specific product you expressed interest in.
  • Respond to direct inquiries.

We do not use your email for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to PeakDownload products.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your email address with third parties.

We may use a third-party email service provider to send communications. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for delivery.

Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you wish to receive updates. You may request removal at any time.

Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us directly.
  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you.

Cookies & Tracking

Our site does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts. We do not track your browsing behavior.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
