Website Information We Collect

Through the PeakDownload website, we may collect limited information that you provide directly, such as:

  • Email address — when you contact us or request product updates.
  • Inquiry details — information you include in messages sent to us.

We do not collect payment information or unnecessary personal data through the general marketing website.

How We Use Your Information

We may use submitted information to:

  • Respond to direct inquiries.

We do not use submitted information for advertising resale, profiling, or unrelated third-party marketing.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. We may rely on service providers such as website hosting, cloud infrastructure, or email delivery providers where needed to operate the business and website.

Data Retention

We retain contact information only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries, provide requested updates, or support legitimate business operations. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

  • Access — request the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data.
  • Unsubscribe — opt out of product updates or communications at any time.

Cookies & Tracking

The PeakDownload website does not use advertising trackers. Limited technical mechanisms such as essential hosting, security, or session behavior may be used where necessary for site operation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
