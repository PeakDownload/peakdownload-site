Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to the Bowling League App, a product of PeakDownload LLC. It describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

Data We Collect

The Bowling League App may collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data — name and email address used to create an account.
  • League and scoring data — scores, game history, player statistics, and league records you enter or that are recorded during gameplay.
  • Usage data — basic application interaction data used to diagnose errors and improve the system.

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the application and provide core features (scoring, league management, standings).
  • To display your statistics and history within the app.
  • To communicate account-related information (e.g., password resets, service notices).
  • To improve the product based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share personal data with third parties except as necessary to operate the service (e.g., cloud hosting providers), and only under contractual data protection obligations.

Scoring and standings data may be visible to other members of your league within the app, as required for core functionality.

Data Retention

We retain account and league data for the duration of your active use. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days. Anonymized aggregate data may be retained for operational analysis.

Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Portability — request an export of your data in a common format.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and access controls. No system is completely secure; we will notify affected users of any data breach in accordance with applicable law.

Cookies & Tracking

The Bowling League App uses session cookies required for authentication. We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via the application or email. Continued use after notice constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions or data requests: privacy@peakdownload.com
