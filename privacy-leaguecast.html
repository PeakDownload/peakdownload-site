Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to LeagueCast, a product of Peak Download LLC. It explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

Data We Collect

LeagueCast may collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data — name, email address, and profile details used to create and manage an account.
  • League data — league setup details, team records, schedules, standings, roster information, and related league activity.
  • Scoring and participation data — scores, match results, game history, player participation, and other records entered or generated through league use.
  • Usage data — limited application interaction and diagnostic data used to support reliability, troubleshooting, and product improvement.

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the service and provide core features such as scoring, league management, standings, schedules, and participation flows.
  • To display league activity, team information, standings, and player-related history within the product.
  • To support account access and service communications, including password resets and important service notices.
  • To maintain, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the product over time.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may use service providers such as cloud infrastructure or hosting providers as needed to operate the product.

League, roster, scoring, and standings data may be visible to other members of the same league when necessary for core product functionality.

Data Retention

We retain account and league-related data for the duration of active use and as needed to operate the product. When an account deletion request is completed, associated personal data is removed within a reasonable period, subject to operational and legal requirements. Aggregated or de-identified usage information may be retained for analysis and product improvement.

Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of personal data associated with your account.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate account information.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Portability — request an export of your data in a commonly used format when reasonably available.

Security

We use reasonable security practices intended to protect data in transit and limit inappropriate access to stored information. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we work to reduce risk and respond appropriately to security issues.

Cookies & Tracking

Website or application sessions may use cookies or equivalent technical mechanisms required for authentication, session continuity, or essential product behavior. We do not use advertising trackers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes may be communicated through the product, website, or email when appropriate. Continued use after an updated policy becomes effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions or privacy-related requests: privacy@peakdownload.com
