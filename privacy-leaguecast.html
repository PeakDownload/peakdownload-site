LeagueCast Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

This policy describes how Peak Download LLC handles information for LeagueCast, a recreational league scoring and operations product.

Information we may collect

  • Account and league profile information provided by users.
  • League, team, player, schedule, scoring, standings, and result information entered through the product.
  • Basic usage, diagnostic, and crash information used to improve reliability.

How we use information

We use information to operate LeagueCast, provide support, maintain league records, improve reliability, and respond to requests.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use league data for third-party advertising resale.
  • We do not collect more information than is reasonably needed for product operation and support.

Service providers

LeagueCast may rely on cloud hosting, authentication, analytics, and crash-reporting services where needed to operate and improve the product.

Retention and deletion

We retain information only as long as reasonably needed for product operation, support, legal, or business purposes. Contact us to request deletion.

Contact

Privacy questions or requests may be sent to privacy@peakdownload.com.
