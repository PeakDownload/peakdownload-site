HomeOps Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

This policy describes how Peak Download LLC handles information for HomeOps, a home operations app for systems, appliances, maintenance, records, reminders, and planning.

HomeOps is designed to organize information the user chooses to enter about their home.

Information We May Collect

  • Home, system, appliance, maintenance, reminder, note, and record information entered by the user.
  • Documents or images the user chooses to attach, where supported.
  • Subscription or entitlement status handled through Apple or the applicable app store.
  • Basic usage, diagnostic, and crash information used to improve app reliability.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the app, provide support, improve reliability, understand feature usage in aggregate, and respond to requests.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use app data for third-party advertising resale.
  • We do not collect more information than is needed for the product experience and support.

Children and Families

Where a product may be used by families or young players, PeakDownload is designed to avoid unnecessary collection of personal information from children. Parents or guardians may contact us with privacy questions or deletion requests.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on platform services, analytics, crash reporting, cloud hosting, payment processing, or app-store infrastructure where needed to operate and improve the product.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information only as long as reasonably needed for app operation, support, legal, or business purposes. You may request deletion by contacting us.

Contact

Privacy questions or requests: privacy@peakdownload.com
