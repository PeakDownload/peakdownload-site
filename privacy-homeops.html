Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to HomeOps, a product of PeakDownload LLC. It describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

Data We Collect

HomeOps may collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data — name and email address used to create and manage your account.
  • Home data — information you enter about your home: address, systems (HVAC, plumbing, electrical), appliances, and maintenance history.
  • Vendor data — contact information for contractors and service providers you add to the application.
  • Usage data — basic application interaction data used to diagnose errors and improve the product.

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the application and deliver core features (scheduling, history tracking, vendor management).
  • To send maintenance reminders based on your home's configuration and schedule.
  • To communicate account-related information (password resets, service notices).
  • To improve the product using aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your home data is private to your account and is not shared with third parties except as required to operate the service (cloud hosting), under contractual data protection obligations.

We do not share your home address, system information, or vendor contacts with advertisers or data brokers.

Data Retention

We retain your data for the duration of your active account. Upon account deletion, your personal and home data is removed within 30 days. Anonymized aggregate data may be retained for operational use.

Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of all personal data we hold.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — request an export of your home and maintenance data.

Security

We use encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), access controls, and standard cloud security practices. We will notify affected users of any confirmed data breach in accordance with applicable law.

Cookies & Tracking

HomeOps uses session cookies required for authentication. We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated in the application or by email. Continued use after notice constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions or data requests: privacy@peakdownload.com
